Grandma Dorothy's Florida Cornbread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 &half cups plain yellow cornmeal
  • 1 &half cups self-rising flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • &half teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 &half cups buttermilk
  • 6 tablespoons bacon dripping or melted butter, divided

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 ° Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl and blend well.
  3. Add the eggs, buttermilk, and 3 tablespoons of bacon drippings and stir till smooth.
  4. Heat the remaining 3 tablespoons of bacon drippings in a 9 inch cast iron skillet over a medium heat for one minute till hot but not smoking. Shake the pan to uniformly coat the bottom.
  5. Pour the cornbread batter into the hot skillet.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes, till brown on top and crispy brown on the bottom.
